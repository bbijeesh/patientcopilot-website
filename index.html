How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complicated setup. PatientCopilot guides you through a simple onboarding and starts working for you right away.

1

Create your secure profile

Sign up in under two minutes. Your identity is protected with a UUID-based account, token authentication, and optional biometric login.

2

Connect your health sources

Link your clinic, pharmacy, and diagnostic lab. PatientCopilot automatically imports your medical history, prescriptions, and upcoming appointments from uploaded copies of your medical document.

3

Meet your AI Copilot

Your personal health agent reads your data and starts surfacing insights, reminders, and answers tailored specifically to you — not generic health tips.

4

Stay on top of your health

Receive timely reminders, track your wellness over time, share records with caregivers, and chat with your Copilot whenever you have a question.

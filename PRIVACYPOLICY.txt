**Effective Date:** 01 April 2026
**Version:** 1.0 (Last updated: 01 April 2026)

This Privacy Policy explains how PATIENTCOPILOT (“Platform”), operated by [Operator Legal Name] (“we”, “us”, “Operator”), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information. It forms an integral part of, and should be read together with, our **User Agreement**.

We act as a **Data Fiduciary** under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.

---

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of data:

* **Account data**: name, email address, phone number, and login credentials.
* **Health Data (sensitive)**: medical records, prescriptions, diagnostic reports, bills, consultation records, and related documents you upload.
* **Family Member data**: information you add about family members or dependents.
* **Usage and device data**: app interactions, device type, operating system, and diagnostic/crash logs used to operate and improve the Platform.

We collect only the data necessary for the purposes described below (data minimization).

---

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your data strictly to:

* Store and organize your medical records
* Provide health insights, reminders, and analytics
* Facilitate sharing of records with persons you authorize
* Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Platform
* Comply with legal obligations

**We never sell your data. We never use your Health Data for advertising or marketing. We never use your Health Data to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models.**

---

## 3. LEGAL BASIS / CONSENT

We process your Personal Data on the basis of your **free, specific, informed, and unambiguous consent**, as required under the DPDP Act. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 8).

---

## 4. HEALTH DATA & SENSITIVE INFORMATION

We treat Health Data as highly sensitive. It is:

* Encrypted in transit and at rest
* Accessible only on a role-based, need-to-know basis
* Never sold, never used for advertising, and never used to train AI/ML models
